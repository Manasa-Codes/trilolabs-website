1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Trilolabs (Trilolabs, we, us, our) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name

  • Email address

  • Company / organization

  • Location / country

  • Project details or inquiries you submit

  • Any additional information voluntarily provided

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you browse the site, certain data may be automatically collected including:

  • Device type, browser type, operating system

  • IP address (may indicate approximate location)

  • Pages visited, time spent, referring links

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you

  • Provide proposals and deliver services

  • Improve website performance and content

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analytics, and performance.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though doing so may affect site functionality.

5. AI & Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools, analytics platforms, communication platforms, and AI technologies to support our services and operations.

Where third-party platforms are involved:

  • They operate under their own policies

  • We do not control their infrastructure

  • We make reasonable efforts to work only with reputable, security-focused providers

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information only when necessary:

  • With trusted service providers assisting in operations or service delivery

  • Where legally required (government, regulatory, or legal processes)

  • To protect our rights, users, and platform security

We do not share personal data for advertising marketplaces or unrelated commercial gain.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, or misuse.

However, no digital transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as required for:

  • Service delivery

  • Legal compliance

  • Business records

  • Security and fraud-prevention

When no longer needed, data may be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Accessing the data we hold about you

  • Requesting correction of inaccurate information

  • Requesting deletion (where applicable)

  • Restricting or objecting to processing

  • Withdrawing consent (where consent applies)

To exercise these rights, contact us via the details below.

10. Childrens Privacy

This website and our services are not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we discover such data has been provided, we will take steps to delete it.

11. International Users

If you access this site from outside our primary operating jurisdiction, your information may be processed in locations where data protection laws differ. By using the site, you consent to this processing.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by the Last updated date above. Continued use of the website after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.

13. Contact Us
